Fixture Generator

Use the form below to generate a concise fixture list for round-robin tournaments in which pairs of players/teams are required to play each either once or twice on a limited number of pitches or courts. Optimal (most efficient) solutions will be sought for 15 seconds, and if none can be reached, the best available will be returned. Players/teams are referred to using numbers 1 - [num_teams], which can easily be substituted for player or team names in a spreadsheet application. Results are delivered on the screen and can be downloaded in .csv format

Note that useful results cannot be guaranteed for difficult queries (group sizes > 20) or nonsensical parameters (groups > teams)!
